Shipping & fulfillment

Fresh meals, delivered on your schedule

Everything you expect from a modern meal service: clear cut-off times, tracking, and safe cold-chain handling.

Delivery windows

  • Sunday & Wednesday evenings — default drops for weekly subscribers (UAE metro).
  • Morning slot (6:00–10:00) — optional for offices and early risers.
  • Evening slot (17:00–21:00) — most popular for home delivery.

Order cut-off

Place or change your order before Thursday 23:59 for the following week’s menu. Changes after cut-off may roll to the next cycle.

You’ll receive a confirmation email and SMS with your delivery day once the kitchen locks your plan.

Coverage & fees

  • Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah — standard chilled delivery (fee shown in pricing).
  • Other emirates — scheduled routes on select days; surcharge may apply at checkout.
  • Building access — leave delivery notes (gate code, concierge) in billing.

Tracking & handoff

On delivery day you get a tracking link when the driver is en route. If you’re unavailable, we can leave the insulated bag with security or a neighbour per your instructions.

Packaging & food safety

Meals arrive in a temperature-controlled bag with ice packs. Refrigerate immediately and consume by the “enjoy by” date on each label.

Returns & issues

Report missing or damaged items within 24 hours through support — we’ll credit or replace on the next delivery.
